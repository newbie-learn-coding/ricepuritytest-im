Rice Purity Score 43 Meaning

Un score de 43 signifie que tu as coché 57 expériences sur 100.

This indicates many selected experiences. It can reflect an active social life, more adult situations, or a history of experimenting with boundaries.

The number should not be used to shame yourself. It can be a prompt to reflect on consent, safety, wellbeing, and whether past choices still fit your current values.

How to read a score of 43

The number is best read as a count-based summary. The score starts at 100 and moves down by one point for each experience selected. That makes score 43 a quick way to say that 57 listed experiences applied to you.

Is a score of 43 good or bad?

No single Rice Purity score is automatically good or bad. The test mixes different kinds of experiences, including relationships, social life, rule-breaking, alcohol or substances, and digital situations. Two people can have the same score for completely different reasons.

FAQ

Does score 43 define me?

No. It is a quiz score, not an identity label.

Can two people with score 43 be different?

Yes. The same score can come from very different selected experiences.

Should I compare score 43 with friends?

Compare only for fun. Do not pressure anyone to reveal private answers.
