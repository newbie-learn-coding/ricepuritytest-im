What Is the Rice Purity Test?

The Rice Purity Test is a 100-question self-graded quiz about life experiences. It is commonly used online as a playful way to compare scores with friends, especially around school, college, and social life.

How scoring works

The score starts at 100. Each selected experience subtracts one point. A score of 72 means 28 items were selected.

How to use it safely

Do not treat the result as a moral judgment. Do not pressure anyone to share answers. Keep it private if the topics feel personal.
