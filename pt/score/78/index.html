Rice Purity Score 78 Meaning

Uma pontuação 78 significa que você marcou 22 de 100 experiências.

This suggests a balanced but still relatively high score. You may have explored parts of social life, relationships, or rule-breaking, while still leaving many checklist items untouched.

The most useful interpretation is not whether the score is high or low, but what it reveals about your boundaries and comfort level.

How to read a score of 78

The number is best read as a count-based summary. The score starts at 100 and moves down by one point for each experience selected. That makes score 78 a quick way to say that 22 listed experiences applied to you.

Is a score of 78 good or bad?

No single Rice Purity score is automatically good or bad. The test mixes different kinds of experiences, including relationships, social life, rule-breaking, alcohol or substances, and digital situations. Two people can have the same score for completely different reasons.

FAQ

Does score 78 define me?

No. It is a quiz score, not an identity label.

Yes. The same score can come from very different selected experiences.

Should I compare score 78 with friends?
