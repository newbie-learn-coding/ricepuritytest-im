Guide

What Is the Rice Purity Test?

The Rice Purity Test is a 100-question entertainment quiz that turns selected life experiences into a score from 0 to 100. A higher score means fewer checked experiences; a lower score means more checked experiences.

The word “purity” is historical. On this site it is not a moral ranking, diagnosis, maturity test, or measure of personal worth.

How the score works

The score starts at 100. Each checked item subtracts one point. If you check 28 items, your score is 72. Your answers are calculated in your browser and are not stored by this site.

How to use it

Use the result as a light self-reflection prompt or a shareable quiz score. Do not pressure anyone to reveal answers or compare private experiences.

FAQ

Is this an official Rice University test?

No. RicePurityTest.im is independent and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by Rice University.

Are answers stored?

No. The quiz is browser-based and does not require a login or account.

Is a low score bad?

No. The score is entertainment only and should not be used to judge anyone.
