Background

Rice Purity Test History

The Rice Purity Test became known as a checklist-style quiz shared among students and online communities. Over time it moved from campus culture into broader internet culture.

Modern versions should be more careful than older wording. Many users take the quiz for fun, comparison, or self-reflection, but the result should never be treated as a moral label.

Why this version is different

This site keeps scoring client-side, avoids login, and uses neutral score ranges. It also explains every exact score from 0 to 100 so users can understand the number without shame.
