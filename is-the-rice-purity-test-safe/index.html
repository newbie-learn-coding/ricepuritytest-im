Safety

Is the Rice Purity Test Safe?

The Rice Purity Test can be safe as a lightweight entertainment quiz when it is private, voluntary, and not used to shame or pressure people.

This version calculates answers in the browser, requires no login, and does not store answer-level data. The result page shares only the score number.

Mature-audience guidance

The checklist includes topics around relationships, substances, rules, and adult situations. It is suggested for mature audiences and should not be treated as advice.

Respect boundaries

Only share your score if you are comfortable. Do not ask someone else to reveal private answers or use a score to judge them.

FAQ

Does this site store answers?

No. Answers are calculated in the browser and are not stored.

Should minors use it?

The quiz is suggested for mature audiences because some topics are adult or sensitive.

Can I share safely?

Share only the score if you want. The share card does not include answer-level details.
