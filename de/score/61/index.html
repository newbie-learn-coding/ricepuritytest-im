Rice Purity Score 61 Meaning

Eine Punktzahl von 61 bedeutet, dass du 39 von 100 Erfahrungen ausgewählt hast.

This sits near the middle of the scale. It often means a broad mix of experiences: some romantic or social exploration, some risks, and many items still unchecked.

A middle-range score is common for people who have had varied experiences but still make intentional choices.

How to read a score of 61

The number is best read as a count-based summary. The score starts at 100 and moves down by one point for each experience selected. That makes score 61 a quick way to say that 39 listed experiences applied to you.

Is a score of 61 good or bad?

No single Rice Purity score is automatically good or bad. The test mixes different kinds of experiences, including relationships, social life, rule-breaking, alcohol or substances, and digital situations. Two people can have the same score for completely different reasons.

FAQ

Does score 61 define me?

No. It is a quiz score, not an identity label.

Can two people with score 61 be different?

Yes. The same score can come from very different selected experiences.

Should I compare score 61 with friends?

Compare only for fun. Do not pressure anyone to reveal private answers.
