Rice Purity Score 18 Meaning

Eine Punktzahl von 18 bedeutet, dass du 82 von 100 Erfahrungen ausgewählt hast.

This is a very low score, meaning most items were selected. It suggests extensive exposure to the kinds of experiences listed in the quiz.

Treat the result carefully. It is not a diagnosis or a verdict; it is a playful quiz that may also encourage reflection about risk, safety, and personal growth.

How to read a score of 18

The number is best read as a count-based summary. The score starts at 100 and moves down by one point for each experience selected. That makes score 18 a quick way to say that 82 listed experiences applied to you.

Is a score of 18 good or bad?

No single Rice Purity score is automatically good or bad. The test mixes different kinds of experiences, including relationships, social life, rule-breaking, alcohol or substances, and digital situations. Two people can have the same score for completely different reasons.

FAQ

Does score 18 define me?

No. It is a quiz score, not an identity label.

Yes. The same score can come from very different selected experiences.

Should I compare score 18 with friends?

Compare only for fun. Do not pressure anyone to reveal private answers.
