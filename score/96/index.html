Rice Purity Score 96 Meaning

A Rice Purity score of 96 means you checked 4 out of 100 experiences.

This usually points to a smaller number of selected life experiences. Many people in this area are younger, more cautious, or simply more selective about relationships, parties, substances, and risk.

Use the score as a reminder that not rushing is perfectly normal. Boundaries and timing matter more than comparison.

How to read a score of 96

The number is best read as a count-based summary. The score starts at 100 and moves down by one point for each experience selected. That makes score 96 a quick way to say that 4 listed experiences applied to you.

Is a score of 96 good or bad?

No single Rice Purity score is automatically good or bad. The test mixes different kinds of experiences, including relationships, social life, rule-breaking, alcohol or substances, and digital situations. Two people can have the same score for completely different reasons.
