Privacy Policy

This website is designed to be private by default. Quiz answers are calculated in your browser. We do not require login, do not ask for a profile, and do not need to store your answers to calculate a score.

What is processed

The browser counts checked boxes and calculates a score locally. If you share a score card, the image is generated on your device.

Analytics and ads

The package ships without third-party analytics or ad scripts. If you add them later, update this policy before launch.
