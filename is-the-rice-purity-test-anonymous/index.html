Privacy

Is the Rice Purity Test Anonymous?

This Rice Purity Test is designed to be anonymous in the practical sense that it does not require a name, account, login, email, or profile.

Your checked answers are processed in your browser. The shareable result link contains only the score value, such as /result/?score=72, not your selected answers.

What you control

You decide whether to download a PNG card, copy an image, use native sharing, or copy the result link. You do not need to share anything.

FAQ

Does the result link include my answers?

No. It includes only the score number.

Can someone see my checked items?

Not from the result link or share card. Do not manually share private answers unless you choose to.
